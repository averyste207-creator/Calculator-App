# Calculator-App
This app is a calculator app. To install follow these steps:

  1) Download the .zip file
  2) Extract the .zip into a regular folder.
  3) Open the quadratic_calculator file inside the folder

Once you open the app, it will automatically open to the quadratic calculator. It gives an explanation of the quadratic formula that it uses. If you enter the a, b, and c values and press calculate, it will give you the solution set. When you enter the side bar, you can change to the basic calculator. The basic calculator has 6 functions. Here are brief instructions for each:

  1) Addition: Put the two numbers you want to add together in the boxes, press the "+" button, and click calculate.
  2) Subtraction: Put the number you want subtracted from in the top box, and the number you want subtracted in the second box. Press the "-" button, and           click calculate.
  3) Multiplication: Put the two numbers you want to multiply in the boxes, press the "✕" button, and click calculate.
  4) Division: Put the number you want divided in the top box, and the number you want to divide by in the bottom box. Press the "÷" button, and click              calculate.
  5) Exponentiation: Put the number you want exponentiated in the top box, and the degree in the bottom box. Press the "^" button, and click calculate.
  6) Roots: Put the radicand in the top box, and the root index in the bottom box. Press the "√" button, and click calculate.
